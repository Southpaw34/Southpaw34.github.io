this website has many helpful resources for playing music on a saxophone, such as:

  • Fingering charts
  • Sheet music for a variety of songs
  • Videos of some of the songs available on the site to help you know how they are played

Of the songs available, there are many different generes to choose from, such as blues, folk rock, and scores from movies and games!
I am planning to keep expanding the library of songs available here, so be sure to check back every now and then to see what is new.
a good place to look for songs is musescore if what you are looking for isn't here.
